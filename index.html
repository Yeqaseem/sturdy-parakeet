About USDU

USDU (United Stable Digital Unit) is a decentralized digital token built to maintain USD parity through a balanced liquidity model. USDU is not affiliated with or a copy of any other USD-titled token, including USDC. The project aims to offer transparency and stable token utility on the BNB Smart Chain.
